Privacy

Last updated: January 2026

Summary

Axilum AI offers two storage modes so you stay in control: Local (this device) or Secure Cloud (account required). The Ask on each upload mode lets you decide case by case.

Storage modes

1) Local (this device)

  • Your conversations and files stay in your browser (local storage).
  • No multi-device synchronization.
  • Important: if multiple people use the same computer and the same browser profile, they may potentially access this data.

2) Secure Cloud (account required)

  • Your data can be synchronized for multi-device access.
  • Access is controlled via authentication (signed JWT token).
  • Strict user isolation on the server (one account cannot access another account’s data).
  • Private cloud storage encrypted at rest (encryption managed by Azure).

What we store

  • Conversations: messages, metadata (depending on enabled features).
  • Files/documents: only if you upload/import them (e.g. Finance invoices/reports).
  • Account: email and information needed for authentication.

Security

  • Encryption in transit: HTTPS communications.
  • Access control: in Cloud mode, access is protected by a signed JWT token.
  • Isolation: each user can only access their own data (in Cloud mode), including files (invoices/reports) and Finance history.
  • Private storage: file containers are non-public and list/download/delete operations are restricted to your account scope.
  • Upload limits: size and formats may be limited to reduce risks.

Retention period

Retention depends on the chosen mode (Local vs Cloud) and your actions (manual deletion). Some data may be kept to ensure proper operation (e.g. history, synchronization), unless you delete it.

Finance module (specific)

When you use Finance features with Cloud storage, conversations and settings are linked to your account, and documents (invoices/reports) are stored in a user-isolated space. This prevents another account from listing, downloading, or deleting your documents.

Your choices and controls

  • You can choose the storage mode in Settings → Data management.
  • You can export your conversations (if the option is available).
  • You can delete your local data from within the application.
